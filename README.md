# Weapon Infusion

A Minecraft mod that adds **tinctures** — a new consumable type that applies potion effects to enemies through weapon attacks.

## Features

- **Tinctures** — drinkable items that stack to 16 and consume 2x faster than regular potions
- **Weapon coating** — having a tincture in your inventory automatically applies its effect to entities you hit
- **Shortened duration** — tincture effects last half as long as their potion counterparts
- **Full vanilla potion coverage** — all 27 vanilla potions have a tincture variant

## Requirements

- Minecraft 26.2
- [Balm](https://modrinth.com/mod/balm) (same version)

## Download

Available on [CurseForge](https://curseforge.com) and [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com).
